What it does
📄
Paste the Job Description
Upload a JD, paste raw text, or drop a URL. The interviewer reads the role requirements and tailors every question, coaching tip, and scoring lens to that specific job.
Role-specific
🎭
5 Interviewer Personalities
Warm, Technical, Adversarial, HR/STAR, Curious. Each has a distinct voice, follow-up style, and tolerance for vague answers. Panel mode splits the question across multiple voices.
Panel · 1:1
🧠
2 Follow-up Questions
Each question runs a full 3-turn exchange: your answer, a follow-up that probes what you glossed over, then a final challenge. The score covers the whole conversation, not just the opener.
Deep probe
📊
Consistent 4D Scoring
Structure. Confidence. Technical Depth. Role Readiness. Every question scored identically. A 7 means solid. A 9 means exceptional. Not inflated.
Session Summary
🔑
Your Key. Your Data.
Anthropic, OpenAI, or Groq (free tier). Nothing stored. Nothing sent anywhere except the AI API. Key cleared from memory on exit.
Security First
🔄
The Feedback Loop
After the real interview — did you get the job? Your outcome (anonymised) helps calibrate what answers actually correlate with getting hired.
